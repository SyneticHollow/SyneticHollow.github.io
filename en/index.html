Panic At The Restaurant

Panic At The Restaurant is a 3D cook and panic game with a top down point of vue, with local co-op. My ambition was to develop a funny game people could play with their friends from 1 to 4 players in a «Overcooked» kind of game.

Scénario

During covid, restaurants are forced to close their doors one by one. On the verge of a crisis, the restaurant 'Les potits clandestins' realizes the opportunity it has. Why not push their underground name to the limit by opening their restaurant despite the restrictions they'r facing. Take control of this restaurant and turn it into a "must see" in the city. Work alone or in a team of 2 to 4 players to welcome customers, take their orders, cook the best dishes, all while respecting precise timings. Don't panic during rush hours!

My work

Along this project, I've developed all of the game’s features & mecanics, such as the interaction between players and ingredients (pick up / drop, cutting, cooking), customer's order creation and delivering, the scoring system, the difficulty, the timer, etc.. I've worked on the 3C, the animations and menu designs. I've also implemented all of the game's feedbacks. And since I like video editing, I've done the trailer. I've basically developed the whole game and I loved it!

Having developed this whole game during 3 month made me progress a lot in so many game development aspect and tools. I've improved my knowledges in C#, Unity, Photoshop and I developed my learn and research skills. I'd be soooo happy to show you more details about the game because I'm so proud of this project! So feel free to ask me more about it :)

Under The Dam

It’s a 2D point&click / strategy / management game about a beaver’s colony wanting to take revenge on humans. To do so they’ll need to gather ressources and attack villages. It has a procedural map that recreates itself each restart.

My work

During this project, I’ve worked on the beaver’s ressources inventory side and the impact ressources have on beaver’s and human’s mood which leads to the game’s win or loss. I’ve also worked on some of the visuals showing human’s and beaver’s respective mood.

This project allowed me to work as a team with designers and to use SCRUM method for game development.

Date : Fall session 2021
