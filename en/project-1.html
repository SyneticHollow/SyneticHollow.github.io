Gym Helper

Date : Fall session 2021

Mobile app that aims to help people managing their gym trainings.
Features :
-Calendar with your personal trainings
-A complete documentation and informations of existing exercices you can add to your personnal trainings
-Recording of the weights and series/repetitions of your exercices.
-History of your previous training sessions
Developed on Android Studio in KOTLIN and XML using Firebase for the data storage part (Firestore)

My work

I was working alone on this project, I had to develop the whole collection architecture of the database. I also developed Gym Helper's features and UI. I would love to show you more about this project if you want to hear more about it :)
