Escape Project

Date : 2018/2019

Escape project is the first game I worked on. It is a game based on the escape games, the player is plunged into a first room in the dark, he will have to find the key to get out of the first room by solving riddles, to then have access to other rooms of the house until he manages to escape.
It's a PC 3D game with a first person pov with mouse/keyboard controls.
It was also the first project I had done on Unreal Engine.
